FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Plora?

Plora is your digital guidebook for climbing and bouldering. It provides all necessary information about the area, the routes and boulders.

Why should I use Plora?

There are multiple reasons you should: content of hundreds of guidebooks, always up-to-date data, in-area navigation, beautiful visualisation and valuable information.

How can I use Plora?

Plora will be available as mobile app for Android and IOS as well as web app.

When will Plora be available?

We are currently working on the first version and will release it in 2023. Stay tuned!

What does the name Plora stand for?

Plora is a combination of the words 'explore' and 'nature'.

Where can I get more information?

Just contact us - we are always happy to about feedback, new ideas and improvements.
